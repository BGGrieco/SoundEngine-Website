Brian Grieco starts his journey in sound at Pulse Recording College in Dublin. After graduating with honours in 2008, he started freelancing as a one man live service provider and location recorder.

Over the next few years, he worked as a mixing/mastering engineer, studio engineer and acoustic consultant. He ran his own sound engineering course, offering a FETAC qualification, from 2009 to 2013.

He started working in the film industry in 2014, as a location recorder and ADR recorder, then as a composer and sound designer, winning recognition for his work in various festivals, including the Clifden Film Fest and Fright Night Film Fest.

In 2015 he attended a course in DkIT with focus on composition and sound design for games, obtaining the level 8 diploma with a 1/1 and starting to offer his services to the game industry.

Today, Brian continues to offer his services to the film and game industries while cultivating a number of personal projects.
